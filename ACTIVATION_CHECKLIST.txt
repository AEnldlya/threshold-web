📅 WHAT HAPPENS

7:00 AM (Automated)
└─ daily_prospect_report.py runs
   - Loads 167 Boston companies
   - Selects top 50 by confidence
   - Groups by category (restaurants, salons, plumbing, etc.)
   - Shows any responses from previous days
   - Generates beautiful formatted report
   - Saves to daily_report.txt
   - Awaits your approval

You (5 minutes)
└─ Review the 50 prospects
   - Check for any that shouldn't be contacted
   - Look at responses from yesterday
   - Decide: SEND or SKIP

You Say "SEND" (10 seconds)
└─ Triggers email sending
   - Email 1 sent immediately
   - 5-minute delay
   - Email 2 sent
   - 5-minute delay
   - ... continues for all 50 ...
   - Total time: ~4 hours
   - All logged automatically

Throughout Day (Monitor)
└─ Replies come in to your email
   - Gmail automatically organizes with labels
   - You can reply to interested prospects
   - Each response tracked

Next Morning (7:00 AM)
└─ New report generated
   - Previous responses listed
   - 50 new prospects ready
   - Cycle repeats
