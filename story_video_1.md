# Story Video #1: "The Job Interview"

## Title
"I got hired in 3 minutes. That should've been my first warning."

## Script (30 seconds)

**[00:00-02] HOOK**
"I applied for a job at a startup. Got a call 2 hours later."

**[02:05] SETUP**
"Interview was weird. Boss didn't ask about my experience. Just asked one question: 'Are you okay with working nights?'"

**[05:10] TENSION**
"I said yes. He smiled. Too much."

**[10:15] ESCALATION**
"First day, I show up. No one's there. I'm alone in a massive office. Door locks behind me."

**[15:20] TWIST**
"Boss appears on the screen: 'Welcome. You're not the employee. You're the experiment.'"

**[20:28] PAYOFF**
"Three years later, I'm still here. Still don't know what they're testing. But the paychecks clear."

**[28:30] FINAL FRAME**
Text overlay: "Apply today"

---

## Production Notes

**Tone**: Dark, unsettling, but oddly comedic
**Pacing**: Fast cuts, music beats sync with story points
**Visual Style**: 
- Normal office shots (first half)
- Increasingly distorted/creepy (second half)
- Red flags hidden in background

**Audio**:
- AI voiceover (deep, slightly robotic but natural)
- Ambient sound (office, then eerie silence)
- Lo-fi horror music (builds tension)

**Target Platform**: TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels
**Expected Hook**: First 3 seconds must grab (people stop scrolling)
