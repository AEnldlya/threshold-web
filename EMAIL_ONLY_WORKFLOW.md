# Email-Only Workflow
## No Calls, Everything via Email

## THE COMPLETE FLOW

1. **Email campaign launches** (50/day, fully automated)
2. **Customer replies to email** ("Tell me more")
3. **You reply with demo link + benefits** (5 min)
4. **Customer shows interest** ("How much?")
5. **You send pricing + close email** (5 min)
6. **Customer commits** ("I'm in")
7. **You ask for photos** (5 min)
8. **Customer sends photos + info** (24 hrs)
9. **You forward to me** (1 min)
10. **I build website** (2 hours)
11. **I deploy to temp** (30 sec)
12. **You send temp link to customer** (1 min)
13. **Customer reviews** (24 hrs)
14. **Customer pays $500** (via email PayPal link)
15. **You register domain** (5 min)
16. **I deploy to permanent** (30 sec)
17. **You send live link to customer** (1 min)
