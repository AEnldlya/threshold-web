## Your Daily Tasks

### Morning (7 AM)
1. **JEwed sends**: "Today's 15 verified businesses ready to call"
   - Names, phone numbers, categories
   - All verified to have NO website

   ```
   "Hi, is the owner/manager available? This is Andy. 
   I noticed you don't have a website. 
   We just built one for [similar business in your category].
   Interested in seeing a quick example? Takes 2 minutes."
   ```

3. **You track**:
   - Who answered (yes/no)
   - Who's interested (yes/no)
   - Contact info if YES

### When Someone Says YES

1. **You tell me (Ryan)**: "Sarah Chen Salon said YES to $2,500 website"

2. **I will**:
   - Create GitHub issue for the project
   - Brief Samantha to start building
   - Log to Airtable
   - Set up everything

3. **CPA tracks**: The expected $2,500 revenue coming

4. **10 days later**: Website is ready
   - I send you payment link
   - You send to client
   - Client pays or doesn't

5. **When payment arrives**:
   - CPA alerts you: "💰 SARAH PAID $2,500!"
   - Samantha deploys website live
   - You get the next call list

## Stock Market Tasks

### When Stock Watcher Recommends a Stock

**Stock Watcher → You**:
```
📈 STOCK RECOMMENDATION

Ticker: MSFT
Entry Price: $380-385
Target: $430 (12% upside)
Stop Loss: $360 (5% downside)
Risk/Reward: 1:2.4 (excellent)
Conviction: 8/10
Thesis: Azure growing 30% YoY, AI expansion
```

### Your Choices
1. **YES** - "I'll buy it" → Proceed to next step
2. **NO** - "Pass" → Wait for next recommendation

### If You Decide YES

**Step 1**: Execute the trade
- Buy shares through your brokerage (not done by bots)
- Buy at the entry price range ($380-385 for MSFT example)
- You decide quantity (use available cash from CPA)

**Step 2**: Tell Stock Watcher
- "I bought 10 shares of MSFT at $380"
- Stock Watcher logs: entry date, price, shares

**Step 3**: Stock Watcher monitors daily
- Updates current price
- Calculates profit/loss
- Sends weekly updates

### When to Sell

**Stock Watcher will alert you**:

Option A - Hit Target (Good):
```
🚨 EXIT SIGNAL: MSFT

Entry: $380
Current: $430
Profit: +$500 (+13%)

TARGET HIT ✓

ACTION: SELL 10 SHARES NOW
```

Option B - Hit Stop Loss (Cut Loss):
```
⚠️ STOP LOSS: MSFT

Entry: $380
Current: $360
Loss: -$200 (-5%)

STOP HIT ✗

ACTION: SELL 10 SHARES NOW
Cut losses quickly.
```

**You do**: Sell when told, then confirm to Stock Watcher
