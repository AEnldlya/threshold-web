Our story

A family trade, passed down properly

Tom Sullivan started this company in 1998 after 12 years working for one of Riverside's largest contractors. His goal was simple: do the work the way he'd want it done in his own home.

Today, the business runs with Tom's son Danny and two other trusted plumbers, all trained in-house. We're not a franchise. We're not a call centre that dispatches strangers. Every job is done by someone we'd personally vouch for.

We've served over 2,400 homes and businesses in the Riverside area. Many of our clients have been with us since the beginning.
