## Stock Trading (Your Part)

### When Stock Watcher Recommends
```
"BUY MSFT at $380-385, target $430, stop $360"
```

### Your Decision
- **YES** - "I'll buy it" → Go to next step
- **NO** - "Pass" → Wait for next recommendation

### If YES:
1. Buy shares on your brokerage (Fidelity, Robinhood, etc.)
2. Tell Stock Watcher: "I bought 10 shares at $380"
3. Stock Watcher monitors daily
4. Stock Watcher alerts: "SELL NOW - HIT TARGET at $430"
5. You sell on brokerage

**That's it. Stock Watcher handles the rest.**
