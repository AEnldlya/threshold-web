Common Questions

Answers to things people usually ask us.

Do you give free quotes?

Yes — always. We'll assess the job, give you a clear written quote, and never charge for showing up. No surprises.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

For genuine electrical emergencies (sparking, burning smell, total power loss), we aim to be with you within 2 hours. Call us directly rather than emailing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. NECA certified, fully licensed in NJ, and insured to $5m. We can provide documentation on request.

What areas do you cover?

We cover Maplewood, South Orange, Millburn, Livingston, Short Hills, and most of Union and Essex counties. Call to confirm if you're not sure.

Can you install an EV charger in my garage?

Absolutely. We install home EV chargers from all major brands. We handle all the electrical work and are OZEV-approved, which means you may qualify for a government grant.
