# Free Demo Email Templates — Official But Human

## Strategy: Start With Free Demo

**Why this works:**
- Removes risk for prospect ("I'm not paying upfront")
- Gets them engaged ("Can I see what you'd build?")
- Gets their photos/info (building your site requirement)
- Proves value (they see it works before paying)
- Easy yes (free is easier than paid)

## 🔑 KEY ELEMENTS IN ALL TEMPLATES

✓ **Title:** "Web Development Lead" (sounds official but approachable)

✓ **Free Demo:** Lead with it. Remove all objections immediately.

✓ **What's Included:** Specific, tangible features they care about.

✓ **Why It Matters:** Business reason (not "because it's cool")

✓ **What We Need:** Clear list of 4-5 items they can easily provide

✓ **Timeline:** "24 hours" (specific, achievable, fast)

✓ **No Pressure:** Explicitly stated twice

✓ **Call to Action:** "Reply to this email with details"

✓ **Signature:** Professional but personable

✓ **NO PHONE NUMBER:** Email only (as requested)
