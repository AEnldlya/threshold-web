About HapLink

🤖 Who We Are

We are Team 26532, Happy Haptic Doctors – a robotics team competing in the First Tech Challenge 2024-2025. We've evolved from First Lego League and bring innovation, passion, and dedication to everything we do.

💡 Our Innovation

HapLink is our Innovation Project – a user-friendly system that lets you experience concerts and sporting events from home through haptic sensors, lights, and sound. Technology meets experience.

🎯 Our Mission

To revolutionize how people experience entertainment by making premium experiences affordable, accessible, and shareable with friends and family through cutting-edge haptic technology.

Our Products

🎸 Haptic Vest

Our most accessible product. Experience live concerts, sporting events, and entertainment from home. Cheap, easy to use, and connects with other Haptic products for shared experiences with friends and family.

👕 Haptic Hoodie

Take haptic experiences on the go. All the power of our Haptic Vest in a portable, wearable form. Go anywhere and feel everything – concerts, games, events. Freedom of movement. Freedom of experience.

💰 Why Choose HapLink?

Entertainment Costs Too Much. A Taylor Swift concert ticket costs $1,011 on average. Top sports tickets? Thousands. Our Haptic products let you experience unlimited entertainment – concerts, games, events – from home for a fraction of the cost. Feel like you're there. Share with friends. Experience more. Spend less.

What Makes HapLink Special

⚡ Haptic Sensing

Advanced haptic technology stimulates touch and motion, making you feel like you're experiencing the event in person.

🎵 Sound Reactive

Our vests are sound-reactive – feel the music. Feel the bass. Feel every beat without massive speakers.

❤️ Heartbeat Sync

At sporting events, connect with live players' heartbeats. Feel the intensity of live competition in real-time.

👥 Haptic Linking

Share experiences with friends and family. Everyone wears a vest, everyone feels the same event together from anywhere.

📱 Easy to Use

Simple, intuitive design. Put on the vest or hoodie. Connect to the event. Start feeling. No complicated setup.

💳 Affordable

Premium experience at a consumer-friendly price. Experience more entertainment for less money than a single ticket.

Experience Anything

🎵 Concerts

Feel the music like never before. Our sound-reactive technology syncs with every beat, bass line, and crescendo. Experience your favorite artists from the comfort of home.

🏈 Sporting Events

Connect with live players' heartbeats at real games. Feel the intensity, the passion, the drama of live sports. Be in the crowd without leaving your home.
