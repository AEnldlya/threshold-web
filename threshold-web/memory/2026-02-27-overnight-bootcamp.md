## Key Insights Provided

1. **Psychology wins deals** — 7 frameworks (PAS, contrast, loss aversion, social proof, reciprocity, urgency, authority) that make people say yes

2. **Recurring revenue is the business** — $500 build is the loss leader. $40/month × 36 months is the real money.

3. **Systems enable scaling** — You can't scale manually. Build templates, checklists, scripts, then delegate.

4. **Outreach method matters more than message** — Cold calls (30%) > cold email (10%). Door-to-door (40%). Don't struggle with emails if calls work.

5. **Quick close is possible** — 3-day build window from contract to launch creates urgency and keeps quality high.

6. **Your competitive advantage** — Speed ($500, 3 days) vs. agencies ($5K, 8 weeks). You're not cheaper; you're faster + affordable.

7. **Reference other clients** — First customer is hard. By customer #3, you have social proof. By customer #10, you're 70% referrals.
