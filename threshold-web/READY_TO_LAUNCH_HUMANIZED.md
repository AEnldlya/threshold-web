## What Can Go Wrong (And How to Fix It)

**Emails bounce hard (>30%)**
- Our estimated emails were slightly off
- Solution: When bounces come back, call the business for real email, send follow-up
- This is normal for cold email. Not a failure.

**Low response rate (<5%)**
- Email subject line isn't compelling
- Solution: Change it. Test. "How [Company] gets more customers" usually works better.

**People want it but want cheaper**
- They say "$500 is too much"
- Solution: Offer payment plan, or you say no, move to next one
- Some people will always say no. That's fine.

**You're slow responding**
- You take 24+ hours to respond to inquiries
- You lose them. They email someone else.
- Solution: Check emails every 4 hours during business hours. Respond within 2 hours max.

**You're not confident on demo calls**
- You stammer through the pitch
- They say no
- Solution: Practice. Write down your talking points. It's just a 15-minute call.
