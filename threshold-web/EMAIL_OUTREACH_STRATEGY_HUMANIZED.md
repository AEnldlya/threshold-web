# Finding Real Emails — The Reality

You've got 167 businesses and estimated emails for them. The question: how do you find the ACTUAL email addresses?

---

## The Honest Truth

These 167 businesses don't have websites. So their emails aren't sitting on a company website for us to scrape.

Where ARE they though?

1. **Facebook** (most common)
   - Search "Business Name Boston"
   - Click their Facebook Business page
   - Look for "Contact Info" or "Message"
   - Their email is usually there

2. **Google Maps**
   - Search the business on Google Maps
   - Click the listing
   - Look for "Contact" button
   - Some show email if the business listed it

3. **Yelp**
   - Search on Yelp
   - Click business page
   - Email sometimes in "Contact" section

4. **Old School Phone Call**
   - Call the number from Google Maps
   - Say: "Hey, what's the best email to reach you at?"
   - They tell you. Boom. Real email.
