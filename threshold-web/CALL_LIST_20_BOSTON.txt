HOW TO USE THIS LIST
================================================================================

BEFORE YOU CALL:
- Google each business name to check if they have a website
- If they have one, skip them and move to next
- Only call the ones with NO website or minimal online presence

CALL SCRIPT (keep it under 60 seconds):

"Hi, is the owner or manager available? ... Hey [Name], this is 
Andy. I noticed you don't have an online presence and I wanted 
to reach out - we just built a website for [similar business] 
and they're getting great results. Would you be interested in 
seeing a quick example? Takes 2 minutes."

IF THEY SAY YES:
- Get their email
- Send them a real example website
- Follow up next day
- Close on second call

IF THEY SAY NO:
- "No problem, keep my number if you change your mind"
- Move to next business

"We build professional websites for small businesses. Takes 
3 days, costs $500 one-time. You own it forever. Includes 
your photos, menu/services, hours, phone number, Google Maps 
location. Interested in seeing what one looks like?"
