## 🚨 POTENTIAL ROADBLOCKS & FIXES

**"I don't know what to say on calls"**
→ Read OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md Script #1, memorize it, practice it
→ First 5 calls will be awkward. By call 20 it's natural.

**"Nobody's responding to my emails"**
→ Try cold calls instead (30% response rate vs. 10%)
→ Or try door-to-door (40% response rate)
→ Not the message, it's the method

**"I'm afraid of making calls"**
→ Everyone is at first. Do it anyway.
→ Call 5 people. You'll see it's not scary.
→ Rejection is fine. It's just practice.

**"I don't know how to build websites fast enough"**
→ Use the BUILD_CHECKLIST.md exactly
→ Aim for 12 hours per site in your first month
→ By month 3, you'll hit 8-10 hours
→ Speed comes with repetition

**"What if they don't like the site?"**
→ Follow BUILD_CHECKLIST.md (2 rounds of feedback before launch)
→ Show mockup on Day 1, get approval
→ Build on Day 2, get approval
→ Launch on Day 3, they approved every step
→ Approval = ownership = they like it

**"What if I can't get the $500?"**
→ Offer payment plan: 50% now, 50% at launch
→ If they can't afford $500, they're not your customer
→ Move on to next prospect

**"What if I can't close anyone?"**
→ Outreach more (100 contacts/week, not 50)
→ Better outreach method (calls > emails)
→ Better pitch (read ADVANCED_PITCH_PSYCHOLOGY.md)
→ By contact #50, you'll close someone
