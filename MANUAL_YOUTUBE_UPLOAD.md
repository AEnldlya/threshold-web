# Manual YouTube Upload (2 Minutes)

Since the automatic auth is hitting OAuth issues, here's the super simple way:

## Step 1: Go to YouTube Upload Page
Click here: **https://www.youtube.com/upload**

(Or go to YouTube.com → Click your profile icon → Create → Upload video)

## Step 2: Select the Video File

Click "Select files to upload" and choose:
```
/home/clawdbot/.openclaw/workspace/story_video_1_CINEMATIC.mp4
```

(Or drag and drop the file)

## Step 3: Fill in the Details

**Title:**
```
The Job Interview - A Dark Story | AI Story
```

**Description:**
```
I got hired in 3 minutes. That should've been my first warning.

A short dark fiction story about a suspicious job interview that leads to a terrifying discovery.

This is a work of fiction created for entertainment purposes using AI technology. All characters and events are entirely fictional.

---

📌 Subscribe for more AI-generated stories
🎬 New videos every week
💬 What would you have done?

#storytelling #horror #shortfilm #thriller #darkfiction #AI
```

**Tags:** (click Add tag for each)
- storytelling
- horror
- shortfilm
- thriller
- darkstory
- creepy
- mystery
- AI
- scary
- fiction

**Category:**
Select: **Entertainment**

**Age restriction:**
Select: **No, it's not made for kids**

**More options:**
- Language: English
- Captions certification: None needed

## Step 4: Publish

Click the blue **"Publish"** or **"Schedule"** button

**Wait time:** ~30 seconds to 2 minutes for processing

## Done!

YouTube will give you a link to your published video.
