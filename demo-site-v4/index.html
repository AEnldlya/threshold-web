What we do

From a few missing shingles to a complete tear-off and replacement, we handle every roofing need for homes and businesses across Hartford County.

Our story

Three generations on Hartford rooftops

Bob Clarke started this company in 1991 after spending his twenties working for a regional contractor. He wanted to do things properly — no rushing jobs, no cutting corners, no sending people to your home he wouldn't trust on his own.

His son Danny joined in 2009, and his grandson Tyler came on board in 2021. It's a family business in the truest sense. When you call Clarke & Sons, you're dealing with people who grew up here, who know every neighbourhood, and who'll still be around in 10 years if you need us.

We've completed over 2,800 projects across Hartford County. We don't advertise. Every job comes from a referral or a returning customer. That tells you everything you need to know.

FAQ

Common questions

How much does a new roof cost?

It depends on the size, pitch, materials, and condition of your existing deck. A typical single-family home in Hartford County runs between $8,000–$18,000 for a full replacement. We'll give you a precise written quote, free of charge, after a site visit.

How long does a full roof replacement take?

Most residential replacements take 1–3 days. We'll give you a clear schedule before we start, and we clean up fully at the end of each day.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes. We work directly with insurance adjusters and can assist with documentation and damage assessment reports. Many of our storm repair jobs are fully covered by homeowners insurance.

What warranty do you offer?

All our work carries a 10-year workmanship warranty. In addition, we use GAF materials which come with a 25–50 year manufacturer warranty. We'll explain every option when we visit.

What areas do you cover?

We serve Hartford and all of Hartford County, including West Hartford, Glastonbury, Wethersfield, Newington, Bloomfield, Windsor, and surrounding towns.
