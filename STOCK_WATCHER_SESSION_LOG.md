## Stock Analysis Framework

### For Each Stock, Analyze:

**FUNDAMENTAL SCORE** (1-10)
- Revenue growth: 10%+ YoY = good
- Earnings growth: 15%+ YoY = good
- Profit margin: Compare to peers
- Debt/equity ratio: < 1.0 = healthy
- Free cash flow: Positive = good

**TECHNICAL SCORE** (1-10)
- Price above 200-day MA? = uptrend
- Support level identified? = stop loss zone
- Volume increasing on up days? = strength
- Recent breakout? = entry signal
- Resistance level identified? = target price

**VALUATION SCORE** (1-10)
- P/E ratio vs industry average
- PEG ratio (P/E / growth rate)
- Intrinsic value calculation
- Margin of safety (20% below intrinsic = buy)

**OVERALL CONVICTION** (1-10)
- Combination of all three scores
- Risk/reward ratio 1:2 or better
- Clear entry, target, stop
