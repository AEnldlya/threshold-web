## DAY 2 (First Outreach)

### Morning: Cold Outreach (3 hours)

**Choose your method:**

#### Method A: Cold Email (Slower but easier)
- Open your Google Sheet of 30 prospects
- Use Template #1 from OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md
- Customize for each business
- Send 10-15 emails
- Track in Airtable: Status = "Contacted 1x", Source = "Cold email"

**Time:** 15 min per email = 2.5 hours total

#### Method B: Cold Calls (Faster, better conversion)
- Pick 5-10 businesses from your list
- Use Script #1 from OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md
- Call during business hours (9am-12pm or 2-5pm)
- Follow the script
- If they say "maybe" → ask for email, follow up by email
- Track in Airtable: Status = "Contacted 1x", Source = "Cold call"

**Time:** 5 min per call = 45 min total (includes script prep)

#### Method C: Door-to-Door (Best conversion, requires travel)
- Pick 5 businesses near you (if in same city)
- Walk in, use Door-to-Door pitch from OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md
- Get business card or write down contact info
- Follow up by phone next day
- Track in Airtable: Status = "Contacted 1x", Source = "Door-to-door"

**Time:** 1.5 hours (travel + talking)

**My recommendation:** Start with Method B (cold calls).
- Faster than email
- Better conversion than email
- You learn the pitch
- Easy to scale

### Afternoon: Follow Up on Any Responses (1-2 hours)
- Check email for responses
- If someone replied → reply back immediately
- Offer a 15-min call
- Use OUTREACH_TEMPLATES.md Template for email response

### Evening: Update Airtable (10 min)
- Add all contacts
- Update Status
- Update Source
- Add Notes
- Set Next Follow-up Dates (5 days out)

**Your first day of outreach results:**
- 10-15 cold emails OR
- 5-10 cold calls OR
- 5 door-to-door visits

**Expected response rate:** 1-3 people
