# Retention Playbook: Turning $500 into $40/Month Forever

The real money isn't in the $500 website. It's in $40/month × 36 months = $1440 per customer.

Here's how to get there.

---

## THE LIFETIME VALUE MATH

**One-time revenue model:**
- Sell 10 sites at $500 = $5,000
- Done. No more money from those customers.
- **Total: $5,000**

**Recurring revenue model:**
- Sell 10 sites at $500 = $5,000
- 7 of them become maintenance customers ($40/month)
- $40 × 7 customers × 12 months = $3,360/year (recurring)
- After 3 years: $10,000 (upfront) + $10,080 (recurring) = **$20,080 total**
- One customer gives you: $500 + ($40 × 36 months) = **$1,940 lifetime value**

**Why this matters:**
One customer who takes maintenance is worth **4x** a customer who doesn't.

Your goal: Convert 60-70% of new builds into maintenance customers.

---

## THE 40-60 RULE

**40% of customers will naturally want maintenance** (they get it)
**60% need to be sold on it** (you have to explain the value)

This means if you don't actively sell maintenance, you lose 60% of potential recurring revenue.

---

## WHEN TO PITCH MAINTENANCE

**Wrong time:** Day 3 (they just got the site, not thinking about it yet)
**Right time:** Week 2-3 (they've had it live, got their first impressions/leads)

**The pitch (email, after their site is live):**

```
Subject: Quick question about [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

Quick check-in: How's the website looking? Getting any 
interest/calls from it yet?

One thing I wanted to mention: a lot of my clients do a monthly 
maintenance plan ($40/month). Includes:

- 2 content updates per month (photos, text, new testimonials)
- SEO monitoring (checking you're showing up in Google)
- Performance checks (making sure your site runs fast)
- Security updates (keeping you protected)
- Email support (when you need something)

Basically: I handle it all, you focus on your business. 
Most clients find it pays for itself with one extra lead per month.

Interested? Or all set for now?

[Your Name]
```

**Why this works:**
- Not pushy (offers two paths: yes or "all set")
- Specific (lists exactly what's included)
- Quantified benefit (one extra lead = $1000+ value vs. $40 cost)
- Timed right (they've had time to see the site)

## THE MAINTENANCE PACKAGE ($40/MONTH)

### What's Included:

**2 Content Updates/Month**
- Client emails you: "Update the testimonials" or "Add new photos"
- You do it within 2 business days
- Takes 15-30 min per update

**SEO Monitoring**
- Monthly check: "Is your business showing up when customers search for you?"
- Share results in an email
- Takes 20 min/month

**Performance Checks**
- Site loads fast? (it should)
- All links work? (they should)
- Mobile looks good? (it should)
- Takes 15 min/month

**Security Updates**
- Any packages need updating? (happens automatically if on Netlify)
- SSL certificate still valid? (yes, auto-renew)
- Takes 5 min/month

**Email Support**
- Client has a question? You answer within 24-48 hours
- Usually: "How do I change the photos?" or "Is my form working?"
- Takes 10-20 min/month per customer

**Total time per customer:** 60-90 minutes/month

**Your hourly rate:** $40 ÷ 1.5 hours = **$27/hour** (not great)

**BUT:** You're doing 10 customers, not 1. So:
- 10 customers × 90 min = 15 hours/month
- 10 customers × $40 = $400/month
- **Rate: $400 ÷ 15 hours = $27/hour on maintenance**

**Doesn't sound great, but here's the magic:**
- This 15 hours/month is way less boring than outreach
- You have recurring income (predictable)
- Customers are happier (less likely to switch)
- You're upselling premium features ($100-200 one-time)
- You're getting referrals (happy customers tell others)

**At 20 maintenance customers:**
- 20 × $40 = $800/month
- 20 × 1.5 hours = 30 hours/month
- **$800 ÷ 30 hours = $27/hour** (still not great hourly, but stable + predictable + happy customers)

## HOW TO GET TO 70% MAINTENANCE CONVERSION

- No mention of maintenance yet

**Week 2 (After they've had it live):**

- Mention maintenance casually
- "A lot of clients do monthly updates..."

- Position as open door, not pushy

**By Week 4:** 60-70% will have said yes

**The ones who didn't:**
- Add to "nurture" list
- Check in every 6 months
- Eventually some will come back

## CHURN PREVENTION (Keeping Customers)

**Why customers cancel ($40/month):**
1. Site isn't getting results → they blame you
2. They're not using features → they think it's not worth it
3. Better alternative → competitor offers something cheaper
4. Just forgot to renew → no reminder

**How to prevent each:**

### 1. Clear Expectations (At Kickoff)
```
"Here's what happens after launch:
- Week 1-2: Things are quiet (Google takes time to index)
- Week 3-4: First leads usually come through
- Month 2+: Steady stream of leads (1-3/month typical)

If you're not seeing results, let me know and we'll optimize together."
```

**Why it works:** They know what to expect. No surprises.

### 2. Monthly Reports (Show Value)
Every month, send a 2-minute email:

```
Subject: [Business Name] Website Report - February

Hi [Name],

Quick monthly update on your site:

📊 Traffic: [X] visits this month
🔍 Google ranking: You're showing up for [X keywords]
💬 Contact form: [X] submissions
📈 Monthly trends: [up/down/stable]

One thing to try next: [suggestion based on data]

Questions? Reply to this email.

[Your Name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Proves the site is working
- Shows you're paying attention
- Gives them actionable ideas
- Creates touchpoint every month

### 3. Proactive Support (Be Easy to Work With)
```
"Quick idea: based on your traffic, your testimonials section is 
getting a lot of attention. Want to add 2-3 more recent ones? 
Free since it's your monthly update."
```

**Why it works:**
- You're thinking about their business
- You're offering free value
- They feel like you care

### 4. Annual Check-in (Keep It Fresh)
Every 12 months, send a renewal reminder:

```
Subject: Your website maintenance plan renews [DATE]

Hi [Name],

Quick heads up: your website maintenance plan auto-renews on [DATE] 
for another year at $40/month.

If you want to cancel, just let me know by [DATE].

Before renewal, want to talk about anything? Upgrades? Design refresh? 
New features? Happy to chat.

[Your Name]
```

**Why it works:**
- Not a surprise charge
- Opens door for upgrades
- Shows you value them

## CHURN RATE TARGETS

**Month 1-3:** Expect 0-10% churn (people are still happy)
**Month 4-6:** Expect 5-15% churn (some realize they're not using it)
**Month 6+:** Expect 10-20% churn (normal steady state)

**If you're hitting 30%+ churn:** Something's wrong. Maybe:
- You're not providing value (sites not getting results)
- Communication is bad (they don't know what they're getting)
- Competitor is poaching you
- You're not following up enough

**Fix:** Go back to the customer. Ask why they left. Improve.
