# Barber/Service Business Template

**Source:** Twisted Scissors - Hanover, NH

## What This Template Is Good For
- Barber shops
- Hair salons
- Nail salons
- Spas
- Massage therapy
- Personal training
- Any local service business

## Key Copy Patterns

### Hero Headline Formula
```
[What you do]. [Value proposition].
```
**Examples:**
- "Good haircuts. Done right."
- "Clean nails. Relaxed you."
- "Stronger body. Better you."

### Service Description Formula
```
[Service name] - [Brief benefit]
```
**Examples:**
- "Classic Haircut - A solid cut that works for you"
- "Beard Trim - Keep your beard looking sharp"
- "Haircut & Beard - The full package"

### About Section Formula
```
[Name] has been [doing service] in [location] for [X] years.
[What makes them different - 1 sentence].
```

### Review Format
Use real reviews with:
- First name + last initial
- 5-star rating
- Actual quote
- Date

## Color Scheme
```css
Primary: Black (#000000)
Background: White (#FFFFFF)
Accent: Gold (#D4AF37 or similar)
Text: Grey variants for secondary text
```

## Sections to Customize

1. **Hero**
   - Business location
   - Headline
   - Brief description
   - Booking link

2. **Features Bar**
   - Years of experience
   - Location
   - Booking method
   - Walk-in policy

3. **Services (3-4 items)**
   - Service name
   - Description (short)
   - Duration
   - Price

4. **About**
   - Years in business
   - Location
   - Brief story
   - Stats (years, rating, etc.)

5. **Reviews (6 real reviews)**

6. **Hours & Location**
   - Full schedule
   - Address
   - Phone

7. **Footer**
   - Logo
   - Hours
   - Contact
   - Booking link

## What to Avoid
- ❌ "Best barber in town"
- ❌ "Premier salon"
- ❌ All-caps headings
- ❌ "Transform your look"
- ❌ Stock photos
- ❌ Fake reviews
- ❌ Excessive animations

## What to Include
- ✅ Real photos of the shop
- ✅ Real reviews from Google/Yelp
- ✅ Clear pricing
- ✅ Easy-to-find hours
- ✅ Simple booking process
- ✅ Brief, honest descriptions
