# Outdoor/Recreation Business Template

**Source:** Outlook Farm - Norwich, VT

## What This Template Is Good For
- Horse farms/stables
- Ranches
- Outdoor adventure companies
- Campgrounds
- Fishing/hunting lodges
- Farms with agritourism
- Nature preserves
- Equestrian centers

## Key Copy Patterns

### Hero Headline
```
[Business Name]
```
Keep it simple - let the image do the work.

**Subheadline formula:**
```
[What you offer] since [year].
```
**Example:** "Horseback riding lessons, trail rides, and boarding since 1998."

### Introduction Section
```
[X] years of [activity] in [location]

[Business name] has been [doing activity] for over [X] decades. 
We started with [small beginning] and now we have [current state].
```

### Service Description Formula
```
[Service name] - [Simple description]
```
**Examples:**
- "Riding Lessons - Private and group lessons for all ages"
- "Trail Rides - Guided rides through the countryside"
- "Boarding - Full-service horse boarding with indoor arena"

### Land/Property Section
```
[Simple headline about space]

[What animals/people do] in [description of space]. 
We have [acreage] of [location] with [features].
```

## Color Scheme
```css
Primary: Dark green (forest/pasture green)
Background: Cream/off-white
Accent: Lighter green or earth tone
Text: Dark stone/charcoal
```

## Sections to Customize

1. **Hero**
   - Large background image of property
   - Location tagline
   - Business name
   - Brief description
   - Two CTAs

2. **Introduction**
   - Years in business
   - Origin story (2-3 sentences max)
   - Photo of activity

3. **Services (2-3 items)**
   - Photo
   - Service name
   - Brief description
   - Starting price

4. **Feature Image**
   - Wide panoramic of facilities
   - Brief caption

5. **Property/Land Section**
   - Description of land
   - Key stats (acres, facilities)
   - Photo

6. **Contact CTA**
   - Simple invitation
   - Contact button

## What to Avoid
- ❌ "Equine excellence"
- ❌ "Where horses shape lives"
- ❌ "Premier destination"
- ❌ "Lush green pastures for happy horses"
- ❌ "Begin your journey"
- ❌ Flowery, poetic language
- ❌ Stock photos of horses/land

## What to Include
- ✅ Real photos of your actual property
- ✅ Real photos of activities
- ✅ Specific details (acreage, facilities)
- ✅ Straightforward descriptions
- ✅ Clear pricing
- ✅ Easy contact info

## Photo Requirements
You'll need photos of:
- Main property/barn (hero)
- Activities in action
- Facilities
- The land/pastures
- People enjoying the experience

## Technical Notes
- Uses regular `<img>` tags (not Next.js Image)
- Works better for static export hosting
- Images go in `/public/images/[business-name]/`
