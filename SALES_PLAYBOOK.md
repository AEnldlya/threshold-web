# Website Sales Playbook — $500 Starter Sites

## Phase 1: TARGET IDENTIFICATION

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Size:** 5-50 employees (small to micro-business)
- **Revenue:** $100K-$1M+ annually
- **Industries:** 
  - Trades (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, landscaping)
  - Services (salon, tattoo, gym, massage)
  - Local retail (boutique, antique, specialty shops)
  - Professional services (accountant, lawyer, therapist)
  - Restaurants (pizzeria, deli, fast-casual)

### Why They're Perfect for $500 Sites:
- Existing websites: outdated or none at all
- Pain point: "People can't find us online"
- Budget constraint: can't afford $3K-$5K agency sites
- Motivation: need leads, want legitimacy, concerned about competition

### Where to Find Them:
- Google Maps (search "[industry] near [city]" → check who has NO website)
- Yelp (filter by businesses with no website URL listed)
- LinkedIn Local Search
- Chamber of commerce directories
- Facebook business pages (check "website" field)
- Cold walk-ins (best conversion rate)

---

## Phase 2: MVP WEBSITE SPEC ($500)

### What's Included:
1. **Landing page** (single or 3-5 pages)
   - Hero section with business name + tagline
   - Services/products list (3-5 main offerings)
   - About section (150 words max)
   - Contact form + phone number + address
   - Google Maps embed
   - Customer testimonials (you write them based on Yelp reviews)

2. **Technical:**
   - Responsive (mobile + desktop)
   - Fast loading (<2s)
   - SEO basics (title, meta description, local schema)
   - Google My Business integration
   - Contact form (via Netlify Forms, no backend needed)
   - Analytics (Google Analytics snippet)

3. **Design:**
   - Professional template (not custom design)
   - Brand colors (ask them for 1-2 colors or use defaults)
   - Clean, modern aesthetic (v4 or v5 template base)
   - NO animations (keep it simple for quick deployment)

4. **Hosting & Domain:**
   - Domain: help them buy (GoDaddy, Namecheap) OR they provide existing
   - Hosting: Netlify (free tier, fast, reliable)
   - SSL: automatic (free via Netlify)
   - Email: they keep existing or we set up forwarding

### What's NOT Included:
- Custom design (use templates)
- Photography (use their existing photos or stock placeholders)
- Content writing (they provide it or you extract from Yelp/Google)
- Ecommerce (no product catalog)
- Blog
- Video

### Deployment Timeline:
- Day 1: Kick-off meeting (30 min), content gathering
- Day 2: Build site + first draft
- Day 3: Revisions + launch
- **Total: 3 days to live website**

## Phase 3: PRICING MODEL

### The $500 Starter Package:
- **One-time fee:** $500
  - Build cost: ~2-3 hours of work
  - Hosting first year: included (Netlify free)
  - Domain: they buy (usually $12/year)

### Upsells (Month 2+):
1. **Monthly maintenance** ($25-50/month)
   - Content updates (1-2 per month)
   - SEO monitoring
   - Performance checks
   - Google Analytics reports

2. **Premium features** ($100-200 one-time)
   - Second domain
   - Email domain setup
   - Google My Business optimization
   - Yelp integration
   - Photo gallery/carousel

3. **Upgrade package** ($300-500)
   - Ecommerce (5-50 products)
   - Appointment scheduling
   - Email newsletter signup
   - Social media integration

### Revenue Model:
- **Acquisition:** $500/site (20 sites = $10K)
- **Retention:** $40/month × 20 customers = $800/month
- **Year 1:** $10K (sales) + $3.2K (maintenance) = $13.2K
- **Year 2:** $800/month × 12 = $9.6K ongoing
- **Portfolio growth:** 50+ sites by end of year = $2K+/month recurring

## Phase 4: OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Option A: Cold Email (Best for Scale)
Template:
```
Subject: Free website audit for [Business Name]

Hi [Owner Name],

I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website yet — you're losing 
customers to competitors who do.

I just built a professional website for a similar [industry] business 
in [city], and they've already gotten leads through it.

I can build you a website (mobile-friendly, shows up in Google) for just 
$500. Takes 3 days. No long contracts.

Want a quick 15-min call to see if it makes sense?

[Your name]
```

**Targeting:** Find 20-30 businesses per week → expect 10-15% response rate

### Option B: Cold Calling (Best for Conversion)
Script:
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name]. I work with [industry] businesses 
in [city] getting them online. Quick question — do you have a website 
right now?"

[Listen]

"Got it. Here's why I'm calling: [businesses like yours] are losing 
customers because people can't find you. I build professional websites 
in 3 days for $500. Interested in a quick 15-min conversation?"

[If yes → book call]
[If no → "I'll follow up via email anyway. What's the best email?"]
```

**Targeting:** 10-15 calls/day → expect 20-30% to take the meeting

### Option C: Door-to-Door (Highest Conversion)
- Walk into local businesses
- Ask to speak with owner/manager
- Same pitch as above (in person)
- **Conversion rate: 30-40%** (highest of all methods)
- Time: 30 mins per stop
- Target: 8-10 stops/day

### Option D: LinkedIn (Best for Professional Services)
- Search for business owners in target cities
- Send personalized message: "Hey [Name], I help [industry] owners 
  get online. Saw your profile — would love to chat about a quick website 
  project if you're open to it."
- Expected response: 5-10%

## Phase 5: SALES PROCESS

### Step 1: Discovery Call (15 min)
Questions to ask:
- What does your business do?
- Who's your ideal customer?
- What's your biggest frustration with not having a website?
- Do you have photos/content we can use?
- What's your timeline?
- (Skip pricing until later)

### Step 2: Qualification
Do they fit the ICP?
- Yes → Move to proposal
- No (too big, wants ecommerce, etc.) → Refer to agency, stay friendly

### Step 3: Proposal
Show them:
1. Timeline (3 days)
2. What's included (4-5 page mockup)
3. Price: $500
4. Two payment options:
   - Full upfront
   - 50% now, 50% on launch
5. Contract (1 page, simple)

### Step 4: Deposit
- 50% ($250) to start building
- Non-refundable after day 1
- Gives you commitment signal

### Step 5: Build & Launch
- Day 1: Content gathering + build homepage
- Day 2: Build other pages + first revision
- Day 3: Final revisions + launch
- Day 4: Monitor + small tweaks

### Step 6: Upsell
After 2-4 weeks:
- Offer monthly maintenance ($40/month)
- "How many calls/leads have you gotten?"
- "Want me to track that for you?"
- Pitch premium features

## OBJECTION HANDLING

**"Why should I spend $500?"**
"Because you're losing customers who search online and can't find you. 
One good lead pays for this."

**"Can't I just use Wix/Squarespace?"**
"You could, but then you're managing it. I handle all the technical stuff, 
optimization, and updates. It's $40/month and takes 15 min of your time/month."

**"I'm not sure people search for my business online."**
"Let's check. Search '[Your business + city]' on Google. Seeing competitors 
with websites? They're getting those customers right now."

**"I'll think about it."**
"Sure. Can I follow up next week? By then you'll have a clearer picture 
of what you're missing."

**"Your price is too low."**
"It's intentionally low because I'm building volume. In 6 months when 
I have 50 customers, prices go up. But I'd rather work with you now 
at this rate."
