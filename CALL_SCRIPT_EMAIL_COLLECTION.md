## SCRIPT (Keep it Simple & Natural)

**When they answer:**

```
"Hi, this is [Your Name]. I'm reaching out because I help local service 
businesses get professional websites. 

Quick question - what's the best email to reach you or the owner at?"
```

**If they ask more:**
```
"I'm offering a free website demo for [Business Name]. Takes 15 min to review. 
What's the best email to send that to?"
```

**If they say "call us":**
```
"Perfect, I'll email you the demo link instead - what email works best?"
```

**If they ask for more info:**
```
"I'll explain everything in the email. What's the best address?"
```

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Best Times to Call
- **Morning (9-11 AM)**: Owner usually in office
- **Avoid Noon-1 PM**: Lunch break
- **Avoid 4-5 PM**: End of day rush

### If No Answer
- Don't leave voicemail asking for email (spam filter red flag)
- Instead: "Hi, I'll try back. Thanks!"
- Call back 2 hours later

### If Transferred
- Don't repeat the pitch, just say:
- "Hi, I need the business email address. Can you provide that?"

### If They Say "Send to our website"
- Reply: "Great, I couldn't find a contact email there. What's your direct email?"

### If They're Hesitant
- Be honest: "No pressure, just trying to provide a free demo. If you're interested, here's how to reach me: [your email]"
