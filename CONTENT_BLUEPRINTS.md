# Content Blueprints by Business Type

Use these templates to know EXACTLY what content to ask for from each client.

---

## BLUEPRINT 1: PLUMBER / ELECTRICIAN / HVAC

### Page 1: Homepage
**Hero:**
- Headline: "[Business Name] - [City]'s Trusted Plumbers"
- Subheadline: "Fast, Reliable Service. 24/7 Emergency Callouts."
- CTA Button: "Call (XXX) XXX-XXXX" or "Schedule Now"

**Section 2: Why Choose Us**
- Stat 1: "X years in business"
- Stat 2: "X+ jobs completed"
- Stat 3: "4.9★ rating on Google"
- Trust badge: "Licensed & Insured"

**Section 3: Services Preview**
- 6 service boxes (see Services page for full list)

**Section 4: Customer Quote**
- 1-2 testimonials from Google/Yelp

**Section 5: CTA**
- "Need service? Call now: (XXX) XXX-XXXX"
- "Or request a free estimate:"
- Simple contact form

---

### Page 2: Services
**Main Content:**
- List all main services with 2-3 sentences each
  - Example services:
    - Plumbing repairs (leaks, clogs, water heater)
    - Emergency service (24/7 availability)
    - Drain cleaning
    - New installations
    - Maintenance contracts
    - Water damage restoration

**For each service, include:**
- Service name
- 2-3 sentence description
- Why it matters to customer
- CTA: "Call for estimate" or "Schedule"

---

### Page 3: About
**Section 1: Owner Bio**
- Owner's name + photo (optional)
- "X years in plumbing"
- Personal story/why they started business
- Philosophy: "We treat every home like our own"

**Section 2: Credentials**
- License number
- Insurance info
- Certifications/memberships
- Years in business

**Section 3: Service Area**
- List all cities/areas served
- "Serving [City] and surrounding areas"

---

### Page 4: Contact
**Information:**
- Phone number (clickable: tel:)
- Email
- Address + Google Maps embed
- Hours of operation
- "24/7 emergency available"

**Form:**
- Name
- Phone
- Service type (dropdown)
- Message
- Submit

---

### Page 5: Testimonials (Optional 5th page)
- 5-8 Google/Yelp reviews
- 5-star ratings
- Customer names + locations
- What they loved

---

### WHAT TO ASK THE CLIENT FOR:

```
Essential Information:
☐ Business name
☐ Owner name
☐ Phone number
☐ Email
☐ Service address (if applicable)
☐ Years in business
☐ License number(s)
☐ Insurance company name
☐ Certifications/memberships

Photos/Media:
☐ Logo (if exists)
☐ 3-5 photos of work (before/after ideal)
☐ Photo of owner/team (optional)
☐ Brand colors (if preference)

Content:
☐ List of main services (5-8)
☐ Short description of each service
☐ Google reviews (I can pull these)
☐ Service area (cities covered)
☐ Anything else they want highlighted

Preferences:
☐ Website name/domain (or we recommend one)
☐ Any specific pages they want
☐ Color preference
```

## BLUEPRINT 2: SALON / BARBER / SPA

### Page 1: Homepage
**Hero:**
- Image: Nice salon/spa photo
- Headline: "[Salon Name] - [City]"
- Subheadline: "Hair, nails, beauty services in [city]"
- CTA: "Book an Appointment" (links to Calendly)

**Section 2: Services**
- 6-8 service boxes (haircut, color, nails, massage, etc.)
- Each with icon + title + short description

**Section 3: Photos**
- Photo gallery (3-5 before/after or salon ambiance photos)
- Instagram embed (if they have one)

**Section 4: Testimonials**
- 3-4 Google/Yelp reviews
- 5-star ratings

**Section 5: Contact + Hours**
- "Walk-ins welcome"
- Hours of operation
- Phone number
- Address + map

---

### Page 2: Services & Pricing
**Format:**
- Service category (e.g., "Hair Services")
  - Service name - $Price
  - Service name - $Price
- Next category...

**Examples:**
- Haircuts: $40-60
- Color: $80-150
- Nails: $20-50
- Massage: $60-90/hour

---

### Page 3: Team
**For each team member:**
- Name
- Photo
- Specialties
- Years of experience
- Social (Instagram link if they have)

---

### Page 4: Book Online

- Embed Calendly or Acuity Scheduling

- Auto-confirmation email

---

### Page 5: Contact
- Phone (clickable)

- Email
- Simple contact form

## BLUEPRINT 3: RESTAURANT / CAFÉ / BAR

### Page 1: Homepage
**Hero:**
- Photo of signature dish or ambiance
- Restaurant name
- "Come visit us at [address]"
- CTA: "View Menu" + "Reserve Table"

**Section 2: Featured Dishes**
- 3-4 signature dishes with photos
- Name + short description
- "Specialty of the house" vibe

**Section 3: Hours & Location**
- Address + Google Maps
- Hours (with note if hours vary by day)
- Phone
- "Walk-ins welcome" or "Reservations recommended"

**Section 4: Menu**
- Link to full PDF menu OR embedded menu

**Section 5: Testimonials**
- 3-4 Google/Yelp reviews
- Photos of food/restaurant

---

### Page 2: Full Menu
**Format:**
- Appetizers
  - Dish name - $Price
  - Description (optional)
- Entrées
  - Dish name - $Price
  - Description
- Desserts
- Drinks (Beer, Wine, Cocktails)

**Can be:**
- Embedded PDF
- HTML table
- Simple list with prices

---

### Page 3: Reserve / Order
- Links to reservation system (Resy, OpenTable, etc.)
- Link to delivery apps (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub)
- Phone for direct reservations
- "Call ahead for big parties"

---

### Page 4: About
- Story: How restaurant started
- Chef's background (if relevant)
- Cuisine style
- Philosophy ("Farm to table", "Family recipes", etc.)
- 1-2 team photos

---

### Page 5: Contact
- Address + maps
- Phone
- Email
- Hours
- Social media links (Instagram, Facebook)
