## COPY-PASTE FORMULA (If You Want to Make Your Own)

```
Subject: [Problem/Opportunity] for [Company Name]

Hey [Owner],

[What they're missing / Problem they probably face]

[Your solution in 1-2 sentences]

[Price + timeline]

[Question to get response]

[Your name]
```

---

## Tips for Using These

1. **Pick ONE version per category** (1, 2, or 3 depending on your vibe)

2. **Customize the brackets:**
   - [Company] = their business name
   - [Owner] = their first name (if you can find it, otherwise just "hi")
   - [Service type] = what they do
   - [Your name] = your name

3. **Keep it short.** Seriously. 3-5 sentences max.

4. **Be genuine.** Don't oversell. You're offering something useful.

5. **One call to action.** "Interested?" or "Worth talking about?" 
   Not "Click here now!" or "Limited time offer!"

6. **Send from your real email.** Not an automated-sounding address.

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't include your phone number  
❌ Don't say "AMAZING OFFER" or "LIMITED TIME"  
❌ Don't lie about results  
❌ Don't use "Re:" in subject line  
❌ Don't send to people 5 times with different emails  

---

## What DOES Work

✓ Short and simple  
✓ Sounds like a real person  
✓ Identifies a real problem they have  
✓ Offers a simple solution  
✓ Reasonable price  
✓ One honest ask
