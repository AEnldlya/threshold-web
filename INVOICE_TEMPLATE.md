## PAYMENT PLATFORMS (FREE OR CHEAP)

### Option 1: Stripe (Recommended)
- Cost: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- On $500: ~$15 fee
- They pay you, you get notified
- Works internationally
- **Pro:** Low friction for client
- **Con:** Slight fee

### Option 2: Wave (Free)
- Cost: FREE (they make money on other services)
- Create invoices, send to client
- Client pays directly
- Track payments automatically
- **Pro:** No transaction fees
- **Con:** Takes 1-2 days to clear

### Option 3: Bank Transfer
- Cost: FREE
- You give them your bank account
- They send money
- You see it instantly
- **Pro:** No fees, instant
- **Con:** They need your bank info

### Option 4: PayPal
- Cost: 2.2% + $0.30 per transaction
- Easy for clients
- Refund-friendly
- **Pro:** Everyone has PayPal
- **Con:** Fee + they can dispute

### Option 5: Check
- Cost: FREE
- Client mails you a check
- You deposit it
- **Pro:** Old school, simple
- **Con:** Slow (5-7 days to clear)

---

**Recommendation:** Use Stripe or Wave.
Stripe is faster (instant), Wave is free (but slower).

## INVOICE CHECKLIST

Before sending invoice, confirm:

- [ ] Client name spelled correctly
- [ ] Amount is correct ($500 or $250)
- [ ] Invoice number is unique (001, 002, etc.)
- [ ] Due date is realistic (give them 7-10 days)
- [ ] Payment methods are clear
- [ ] Your contact info is correct
- [ ] Services description is accurate
- [ ] Save as PDF before sending

---

**Quick wins:**
- Always include the signed contract reference
- Always list services clearly (so they know what they're paying for)
- Always give them multiple payment options
- Always follow up if they don't pay within 5 days
- Always launch their site only after payment is received
