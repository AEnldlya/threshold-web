## How Animations Work

### User scrolls down →

1. **Title enters view** → fades in smoothly
2. **Cards come into view** → cascade in sequence
   - 1st card: animates immediately (0s)
   - 2nd card: 0.1s later
   - 3rd card: 0.2s later
   - Rest: 0.3s later
3. **Gallery images** → scale in as scrolled into view

### Result
Smooth, professional reveal without flashy effects. Just like Apple.com or Stripe.com!

2. **Timing**: Edit `0.7s` to different duration
3. **Easing**: Change `cubic-bezier(...)` function
