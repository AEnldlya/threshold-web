About HapLink

Who We Are

Team 26532, Happy Haptic Doctors, is a robotics team competing in the First Tech Challenge 2024-2025. We've evolved from First Lego League and bring innovation and dedication to everything we do.

Our Innovation

HapLink is our innovation project - a user-friendly system that lets you experience concerts and sporting events from home through haptic sensors, lights, and sound.

Our Mission

To revolutionize how people experience entertainment by making premium experiences affordable, accessible, and shareable with friends and family.

Our Products

Haptic Vest

Experience live concerts, sporting events, and entertainment from home. Our most accessible product that connects with other Haptic products.

Haptic Hoodie

Take haptic experiences on the go. All the power of our Haptic Vest in a portable, wearable form. Experience freedom of movement and experience.

Why HapLink

A Taylor Swift concert ticket costs $1,011 on average. Sports tickets? Thousands. Our products let you experience unlimited entertainment from home for a fraction of the cost. Feel like you're there. Share with friends.

What Makes Us Special

Haptic Sensing

Advanced technology stimulates touch and motion like you're there in person.

Sound Reactive

Feel the music. Feel every beat without massive speakers.

Heartbeat Sync

Connect with live players' heartbeats at sporting events.

Haptic Linking

Share experiences with friends and family anywhere in the world.
